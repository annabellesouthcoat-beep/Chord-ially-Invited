RICHARD | THE GROOVE

Every powerhouse band needs a foundation that refuses to budge. Richard is the anchor of the Chord-ially Invited sound, providing the driving basslines that link the kit to the guitars.

Richard’s playing is all about the groove. By locking in perfectly with Bella’s kick drum, he creates a tight, rhythmic pocket that allows the rest of the band to shine. Whether it's a driving rock anthem or a soulful pop classic, the energy starts from the floor up.
