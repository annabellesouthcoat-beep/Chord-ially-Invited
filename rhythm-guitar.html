CRAIG | THE RHYTHM

Every powerhouse drum beat needs a rock-solid rhythm to lock into. For Craig, providing that driving, "chunky" foundation isn't just a job—it's a lifelong obsession with the classic rock 'n' roll shuffle.

Craig's musical journey started in the same household as Bella's, where a shared passion for driving energy led him to pick up the guitar. While his brother-in-the-lineup (Ollie) handles the soaring leads, Craig is focused on the "Parfitt-style" wall of sound that makes the Chord-ially Invited setlist feel so unapologetically massive.
