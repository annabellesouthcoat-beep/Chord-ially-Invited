EM | THE VOICE

Every powerhouse band needs a voice capable of cutting through the mix with both soul and grit. Em provides the soaring melodies and infectious energy that keeps the dance floor packed.

Em's ability to transition from 90s pop anthems to driving 2000s rock hits is the secret weapon of the Chord-ially Invited sound. Whether she's channeling the folk-pop roots of The Corrs or the high-octane energy of Paramore, her performance is always about one thing: connecting with the audience.
